Kibbeh Recipe

Kibbeh is a delicious Lebanese traditional food made out of meat and bulgur. Its taste pallet is comforting to all ages and this recipe will make it so hard to resist.

Some of the ingredients needed are of the following:

  • Ground beef
  • Bulgur
  • Onion
  • Salt, pepper, 7 spices

Now that we have listed all that we need, let him cooooook!

  1. First cook the beef and onion and spices in a pan and leave to rest
  2. Next, mix the raw ground beef and bulgur for the casing and add salt and pepper
  3. Finally make your kibbeh balls and fill with the meat mixture
