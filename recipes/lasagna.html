Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is one of the most delicious meals anyone can make. Its taste pallet is comforting to all ages and this recipe will make it so hard to resist.

Some of the ingredients needed are of the following:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Salt, pepper, oregano, paprika

Now that we have listed all that we need, let him cooooook!

  1. First you must boil your pasta until it becomes soft but not too soft.
  2. Next, work on your sauce. Brown the beef and add your spices (salt, pepper, oregano, paprika) according to your own taste
  3. Then add your tomato sauce on top, let it simmer and turn it off
  4. Then you whisk some butter and flour to make a rue, and add milk and salt and pepper
  5. Finally, assemble your lasagna and end with cheese on top
